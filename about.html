About

Every piece is one-of-one.

Vaulted Pieces starts with heavyweight blanks — 350gsm French terry cotton, built to hold up. From there, every garment is hand-painted one at a time. Star-fields, wings, and clouds are laid down freehand, so no two pieces ever come out exactly the same. What you're wearing is the only one like it.

Each piece is washed and tested before it ships, then finished with embroidered detailing and the VP monogram. Drops are small and limited by design — when a size sells out, it's gone, and there's no guaranteed restock.

This is slow, hands-on production, not mass manufacturing. That's the trade we're making: fewer pieces, more attention to each one.
