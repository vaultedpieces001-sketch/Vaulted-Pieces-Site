About

Every piece is one-of-one.

I'm 17, and I've always wanted to make clothes. Not as a side hustle or something to try out — it's genuinely a passion, and Vaulted Pieces is me actually going after it instead of just thinking about it.

Vaulted Pieces starts with heavyweight blanks — 350gsm French terry cotton, built to hold up. From there, every garment is hand-painted by hand, one at a time. Star-fields, wings, and clouds are laid down freehand, so no two pieces ever come out exactly the same. What you're wearing is the only one like it.

Each piece is washed and tested before it ships, then finished with embroidered detailing and the VP monogram. Drops are small and limited by design — when a size sells out, it's gone, and there's no guaranteed restock.

This is slow, hands-on production, not mass manufacturing, and it's just the beginning. I've got big plans for where this goes — thanks for being here early.
