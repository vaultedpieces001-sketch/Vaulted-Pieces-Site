About

Carefully designed. Carefully made.

I'm 17, and I've always wanted to make clothes. Not as a side hustle or something to try out — it's genuinely a passion, and Vaulted Pieces is me actually going after it instead of just thinking about it.

Vaulted Pieces starts with heavyweight blanks — 350gsm French terry cotton, built to hold up. Every graphic is designed by me first, then carefully produced onto each piece. Star-fields, wings, and clouds — every detail is something I actually sat down and designed, not an afterthought.

Each piece is washed and tested before it ships, then finished with embroidered detailing and the VP monogram. Drops are small and limited by design — when a size sells out, it's gone, and there's no guaranteed restock.

This is a small, independent drop, and it's just the beginning. I'm hands-on with the design and every decision behind it, with big plans for where this goes — thanks for being here early.
