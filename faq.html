FAQ

Everything you need to know before you order.

Is every piece really one-of-one?

Yes. Each garment is hand-painted individually, so the placement, texture, and finish of the star-field and wing graphics vary slightly from piece to piece. What you receive is the only one exactly like it.

What sizes are available?

The Midnight drop runs S, M, L, and XL. Sizing follows a relaxed, cropped fit for the hoodie and a tapered fit for the sweatpants. If a size is unavailable on a product page, it's sold out for this drop.

Will you restock sold-out sizes?

Drops are intentionally limited. We don't guarantee restocks — once a size sells out, it may not come back. Join the SMS list to get notified first if more become available.

How do I care for a hand-painted piece?

Every piece is washed and tested before it ships. We recommend washing cold, inside out, and avoiding high-heat tumble drying to help the hand-painted graphic last as long as possible.

What's your shipping and returns policy?

All sales are final — because each piece is hand-painted and one-of-one, we're not able to accept returns or exchanges for sizing or preference. That said, if something arrives damaged, defective, or wrong, email us at vaultedpieces001@gmail.com and we'll make it right.

How do I get early access to future drops?

Sign up for the SMS list on the homepage. Subscribers get first access to new drops, restock alerts, and exclusive discounts.
